App Design

I was tasked to create the logo for an upcoming e-commerce app catered towards parents with children from 1 - 5 years of age. I went with the name Parallel Play, as it is one of the development processes that children of said age undergo with catchy alliteration. Triangles, being one of the first shapes babies recognise, as well as the basic colours of red and blue, are used.
